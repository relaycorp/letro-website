Account deletion

To delete a free account under a Relaycorp-managed domain name (e.g., applepie.rocks, cuppa.fans), simply delete the account from the Settings screen in Letro.

To delete an account under a domain managed by a third-party, delete account from the Settings screen in Letro and then contact that third party.
